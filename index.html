Is Wealth Vintage safe to use?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, OTP verification on every login, and PIN protection. Your funds and data are protected at all times.

How do I open an account?

Click "Open Account", fill in your basic details, verify your email with a one-time code, and your account is live — all under five minutes, no paperwork required.

What transfer methods are supported?

We support local bank transfers, international wire, PayPal, Wise, Bitcoin, Cash App, Zelle, Venmo, Revolut, and more across 180+ countries.

Are there fees for transfers?

Local transfers are completely free. International transfers carry minimal fees depending on destination — all clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction.

Can I use Wealth Vintage on mobile?

Yes — fully responsive on all devices. A dedicated native mobile app is also in development and coming very soon.

How do I reach support?

Via the live chat widget in your dashboard or our Contact page. Real people available 24/7 — no bots.
